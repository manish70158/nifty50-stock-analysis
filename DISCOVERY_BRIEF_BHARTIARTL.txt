COMPANY OVERVIEW:
-----------------
Company Name: Bharti Airtel Limited
Ticker: BHARTIARTL.NS
Exchange: NSE (National Stock Exchange of India)
Sector: Telecommunications
Industry: Telecom Services

Business Description:
Bharti Airtel Limited is India's leading telecommunications services provider with
operations across 18 countries in South Asia and Africa. The company provides:
- Mobile services (prepaid and postpaid)
- Fixed broadband and fiber services
- Digital TV services (DTH)
- Enterprise solutions and cloud services
- Digital payments and financial services

Headquarters: New Delhi, India
Founded: 1995
IPO Year: 2002
CEO: Gopal Vittal (India & South Asia CEO)
Employees: Approximately 30,000+

Key Business Segments:
1. Mobile Services (India) - Primary revenue driver
2. Airtel Business (Enterprise Services)
3. Homes (Broadband & DTH)
4. Digital Services (Airtel Payments Bank, Wynk Music, etc.)
5. Africa Operations (present in 14 African countries)

MARKET POSITION:
----------------
- #2 telecom operator in India by subscribers (after Jio)
- Leading player in premium postpaid segment
- Strong presence in enterprise and SMB segment
- Largest telecom operator in Africa by geography
- Key competitor to Reliance Jio and Vodafone Idea in India
