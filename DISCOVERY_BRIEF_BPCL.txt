SECTION 2: COMPANY OVERVIEW
------------------------------------------
Company Description:
Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL) is India's second-largest public sector oil refining and marketing company. The company operates through three main business segments: Refining (processing crude oil into petroleum products), Marketing (retail fuel distribution through 19,000+ outlets), and Gas (LPG distribution and city gas).

Key Business Segments:
1. Refining - 3 major refineries with combined capacity of 38 MMTPA
   - Mumbai Refinery: 12 MMTPA
   - Kochi Refinery: 15.5 MMTPA
   - Bina Refinery (JV): 7.8 MMTPA

2. Marketing - Largest retail network in India
   - 19,000+ fuel retail outlets
   - Market share: ~23% of petroleum products
   - Aviation fuel supply to 67+ airports

3. LPG & Gas - Leading household LPG distributor
   - 7,500+ LPG distributorships
   - City Gas Distribution (CGD) projects in multiple cities

Leadership:
- Chairman & MD: G. Krishnakumar (since 2022)
- Government Ownership: 52.98% (Public Sector Undertaking)
- Established: 1952 (as Burmah Shell)
- Employees: ~11,500+
- Headquarters: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Key Strategic Initiatives:
- Digital transformation (Fuel@Click, SmartFleet)
- Expansion of EV charging infrastructure
- Renewable energy investments (solar, wind, biofuels)
- Petrochemical capacity expansion
