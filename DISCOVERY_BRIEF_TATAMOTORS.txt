BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
--------------------
Tata Motors is India's largest automobile manufacturer and a leading global automotive company. The company operates through two main segments:

1. Automotive Operations:
   - Passenger Vehicles: Includes popular models like Nexon, Harrier, Safari, Tiago, Punch
   - Commercial Vehicles: Market leader in India with trucks, buses, and light commercial vehicles
   - Jaguar Land Rover (JLR): Owns the premium British automotive brands
   - Electric Vehicles: Strong push with Nexon EV, Tiago EV, and upcoming EV portfolio
   - Defense Vehicles: Supplies to Indian armed forces

2. All Other Operations:
   - Construction equipment
   - Vehicle financing (Tata Motors Finance)
   - Information technology services

Global Presence: Operations in India, UK, Europe, Asia, Africa, and internationally.
