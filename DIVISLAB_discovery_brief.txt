BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
Divi's Laboratories Limited is a leading Indian pharmaceutical company specializing in the manufacture and sale of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs), intermediates, and nutraceutical ingredients. The company operates globally with presence in India, North America, Asia, Europe, and internationally. Divi's engages in custom synthesis contract manufacturing services of APIs and intermediates, and supplies a range of carotenoids (beta carotene, astaxanthin, lycopene, canthaxanthin), lutein, and vitamins used in food, beverage, dietary supplement, and pet nutrition industries. The company is known for its backward integration capabilities, quality manufacturing, and strong relationships with global pharma companies.

KEY PRODUCTS & SEGMENTS:
1. Generic APIs - Cardiovascular, anti-diabetic, anti-asthma, anti-cancer medications
2. Custom Synthesis & Contract Manufacturing - Proprietary drug intermediates for innovator pharma companies
3. Nutraceutical Ingredients - Carotenoids, vitamins, and dietary supplement ingredients
4. Key Therapeutic Areas: CNS, Cardiovascular, Gastro-intestinal, Anti-diabetic, Anti-inflammatory
