## Executive Summary

**Company**: Hindalco Industries Limited is India's largest aluminum producer and one of Asia's largest copper manufacturers, operating as a flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group. Through its wholly-owned subsidiary Novelis Inc., Hindalco is also the world's largest aluminum rolling and recycling company, providing significant global exposure to automotive, beverage packaging, and specialty markets.
