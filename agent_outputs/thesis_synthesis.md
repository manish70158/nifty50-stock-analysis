### 1. CORE THESIS

**"Positioned for India's Infrastructure-Led Growth Cycle with Operational Scale and Cost Leadership"**

JSW Steel represents a **multi-year play on India's infrastructure buildout and manufacturing growth**, offering investors exposure to domestic steel consumption growth (6-8% CAGR) through a well-managed, efficiently-run steel producer. The company is in the midst of aggressive but disciplined capacity expansion (18 MTPA → 37 MTPA by 2030) that will enable it to capture market share as India's per capita steel consumption rises from low base (~70 kg) toward developed market levels (300-500 kg). While cyclical risks remain significant, the combination of **fair valuation, cost leadership from coastal plants, deleveraging trajectory, and India's secular growth story** creates a reasonable risk/reward for patient capital.

**Why This Stock:** Leading Indian steel producer with scale, efficiency, and growth runway in underpenetrated domestic market.

**Why Now:** Mid-cycle positioning with valuation not demanding perfection, ahead of multi-year infrastructure spending and capacity expansion payoff.

**What Is The Edge:** Understanding that steel is cyclical but India's structural growth story is not; buying quality cyclical at reasonable valuation before catalyst convergence.
