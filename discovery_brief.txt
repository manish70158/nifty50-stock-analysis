COMPANY OVERVIEW
Company Name: Power Grid Corporation of India Limited
Description: Power Grid Corporation of India Limited is India's largest electric power transmission utility. The company operates in three segments: Transmission Services (core business), Telecom Services, and Consultancy Services. As of March 31, 2025, POWERGRID owns and operates 180,195 circuit kilometers of transmission lines and 282 substations with aggregate transformation capacity of 551,961 MVA. The company is critical infrastructure for India's power sector, transmitting approximately 50% of total power generated in India.
