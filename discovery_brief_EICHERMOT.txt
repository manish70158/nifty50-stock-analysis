Business Description:
Eicher Motors is an Indian automobile company specializing in the manufacture and sale of motorcycles and commercial vehicles. The company owns the legendary Royal Enfield brand, which dominates the premium motorcycle segment in India. Royal Enfield offers a diverse portfolio including Classic 350, Bullet 350, Meteor 350, Hunter 350, Himalayan 450, Scram 440, Interceptor 650, Bear 650, Guerrilla 450, Continental GT 650, Super Meteor 650, and Shotgun 650 models. The company also manufactures motorcycle accessories, protective gear, riding apparel, and lifestyle products for men and women.

Key Products & Revenue Segments:
1. Royal Enfield Motorcycles (Primary revenue driver)
   - Classic Series (350cc) - High volume segment
   - Adventure Series (Himalayan 450, Scram 440) - Growing segment
   - Premium Series (650cc twins) - High margin segment
2. Accessories & Apparel
3. International Markets (expanding presence)
