## COMPANY OVERVIEW
Company Name: JSW Steel Limited
Ticker: JSWSTEEL.NS
Exchange: National Stock Exchange of India (NSE)
Sector: Basic Materials / Steel & Iron
Industry: Steel Manufacturing

Business Description:
JSW Steel Limited is India's leading integrated steel manufacturer and part of the USD 24 billion JSW Group. The company manufactures flat-rolled steel products including Hot Rolled (HR) coils and sheets, Cold Rolled (CR) coils and sheets, Galvanized (GP/GC) products, Color Coated products, and Steel Pipes & Tubes. JSW Steel serves automotive, construction, infrastructure, energy, and engineering sectors. The company operates multiple integrated steel plants across India with significant capacity expansion plans.

Key Products & Revenue Segments:
- Flat Steel Products (Hot Rolled, Cold Rolled, Galvanized)
- Coated Steel Products
- Steel Pipes and Tubes
- Specialty Steel Products
- Long Steel Products (through subsidiaries)

Leadership:
- Chairman: Sajjan Jindal (Founder and key promoter)
- Joint Managing Director & CEO: Jayant Acharya
- Headquarters: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
- Employees: ~40,000+ employees
- IPO: Listed since 1994
