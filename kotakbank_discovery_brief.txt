BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
Kotak Mahindra Bank is one of India's leading private sector banks and part of the Kotak Mahindra Group. The bank was established in 2003 after converting Kotak Mahindra Finance Ltd (founded 1985) into a commercial bank. It offers comprehensive financial services including retail banking, corporate banking, investment banking, life insurance, asset management, general insurance brokerage, and stock broking. The bank is known for its strong asset quality, digital innovation, and diversified business model across banking and financial services subsidiaries.
