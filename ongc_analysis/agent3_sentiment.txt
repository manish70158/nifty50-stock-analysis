**SENTIMENT VERDICT:**
ONGC faces a **"Show Me" sentiment environment**. Market participants are skeptical but not aggressively bearish. The stock needs to prove itself through:
1. Operational delivery (stabilizing production)
2. External catalyst (crude price rally)
3. Policy positive (govt easing up)

Without these catalysts, sentiment likely remains muted, keeping multiples compressed despite deep value. This is a **"value stock waiting for a catalyst"** - sentiment will follow fundamentals, not lead them.
