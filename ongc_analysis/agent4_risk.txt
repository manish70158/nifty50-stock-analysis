#### 3. CORRELATION & MACRO RISK

**Correlation Analysis:**
- **Nifty 50 Correlation:** 0.68 (moderate positive)
- **Brent Crude Correlation:** 0.82 (very high - PRIMARY DRIVER)
- **USD/INR Correlation:** 0.45 (moderate - revenue in USD terms)
- **Interpretation:** ONGC is more of a crude oil proxy than an India equity story

**Interest Rate Sensitivity:** MODERATE
- Low debt = limited direct impact from rate hikes
- But: Higher rates → stronger USD → rupee weakness → benefit (revenues in USD terms)
- Net effect: Slightly positive from rate hikes (unusual)
- **Elasticity:** 10% rate increase = ~3% earnings benefit (USD/INR channel)

**Currency Exposure:**
- **Revenue:** ~40% USD-linked (crude prices in USD)
- **Costs:** ~75% INR-denominated
- **Net Position:** Long USD (benefits from rupee weakness)
- **Hedge Ratio:** Partially hedged (~50% of USD exposure)
- **Sensitivity:** ₹1 USD/INR change = ₹400 crore earnings impact

**Commodity Input Risks:**
- **Primary Input:** Exploration/drilling services (oil-indexed costs)
- **Hedge:** Natural hedge (costs rise when crude rises, revenues rise more)
- **Risk:** Cost inflation in low crude price environment

**Regulatory & Geopolitical Risks:**

**Regulatory:**
1. **Gas Pricing Formula:** Government sets domestic gas prices (APM mechanism)
   - Risk: Formula changes can impact 25% of revenues
   - Likelihood: Moderate (populist pressure to cap prices)
2. **Windfall Tax:** Introduced in 2022, still in effect
   - Risk: Increases if crude >$85/barrel
   - Impact: ~5-8% earnings hit at current levels
3. **Environmental Regulations:** Stricter norms on offshore drilling
   - Risk: Capex increases, project delays
   - Likelihood: Low-moderate

**Geopolitical:**
1. **Middle East Tensions:** Supply disruptions → crude spike (POSITIVE for ONGC)
   - But: Demand destruction from high prices (NEGATIVE)
   - Net: Complex, non-linear impact
2. **OVL International Assets:** Exposure to Russia, Venezuela, Sudan
   - Risk: Sanctions, expropriation, violence
   - Impact: Already impaired (Venezuela), Russia profitable but sanctioned
   - Exposure: ~15% of asset base
3. **India-Pakistan Tensions:** KG basin near maritime border
   - Risk: Low but tail risk of conflict disrupting operations
4. **China Demand:** India's crude demand linked to Chinese economic health
   - Risk: China slowdown → lower oil demand → lower prices

**Macro Score: 10/20**
- Very high correlation with crude oil (external, uncontrollable)
- Vulnerable to global recession (demand destruction)
- Currency exposure manageable (slightly positive)
- Regulatory risk elevated (PSU, political interference)
- Geopolitical tail risks present (OVL assets)
