#### 1. CORE THESIS

**The Case in 3 Sentences:**

ONGC presents a **tactical deep value opportunity** for contrarian investors who believe crude oil will rally above $85/barrel in the next 12-18 months, triggering multiple expansion from the current 6.8x P/E (40% discount to historical average) back toward 9-10x while generating 6%+ dividend yield during the wait. The stock has been oversold due to a confluence of temporary factors (production decline narrative, government policy uncertainty, ESG-driven FII selling) that mask the underlying value of a fortress balance sheet generating ₹24,500 crore in annual free cash flow. However, this is a **"prove it" story** requiring crude oil cooperation and operational execution—not a high-conviction long-term hold, but rather a 12-24 month tactical position with defined entry (₹205-212), stop (₹195), and targets (₹240/₹270).
